Simple Budget Calculator for the Young Adult and College Student

As you transition from being a high school student to becoming a young adult, it is essential for you to become familiarized with a budget. By having a budget, it allows you to become financially independent, financially stable, and be a self-sufficient individual. However, setting a budget does not have to be a difficult task. The key ingredient to a successful budget is keeping a consistancy every month.More importantly,however, it is vital to realize that your budget should be a realistic accomplishment. Saving every penny it is not always a satisfying lifestyle.

To begin this budget, you should list all the items that you purchase in a month. Some items that you purchase every month will be fixed such as rent, utilities, car insurance, cable bill, credit card payment, bus pass, gym membership,or student loan. Other items, however, are not fixed and will vary depending on how much you contribute on its spending. Some examples of these items are gas, groceries, dinning out, textbook purchases, and miscellaneous spending.
